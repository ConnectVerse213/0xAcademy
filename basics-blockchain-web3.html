Basics of Blockchain and Web3

Learn the fundamentals of Blockchain and Web3 from the basic level and build a web3 project


Course Overview

This comprehensive course introduces you to the world of blockchain technology and Web3. You'll learn the core concepts, understand how decentralized networks operate, and gain hands-on experience building your own blockchain project.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of blockchain technology
  • How cryptocurrencies work
  • Smart contracts and decentralized applications
  • Web3 architecture and protocols
  • Hands-on project development
